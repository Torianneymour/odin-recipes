Protein Smoothie

Powerful and simple to make!

This is a simple yet effective smoothie that has a good amount of protein and fiber.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Milk
  • Banana
  • Peanut Butter

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients into blender.
  2. Let blend for roughly 10 seconds.
  3. Poor into your prefered cup and enjoy!

You'll notice this smoothie acts as a powerful meal replacement that gets you up and at it in the morning time.
