Cracked Conch

A must have Bahamian Dish!

Cracked Conch is a must-have dish, when visiting the Bahamas. Luckily for us Bahamians aren't the only ones who can make cracked conch.

Below we have everything you need to make your very own cracked conch and enjoy it's fluffy and chewy delight.

Ingredients

  • Conch
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Complete Seasoning
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Rinse Conch meat with warm water.
  2. Cut Conch into long flat pieces (however best you can).
  3. Using meat tenderizer, beat conch until it is soft and broken up into small pieces that are barely linked together
  4. Season conch with complete seasoning and then place in a container of milk to marinate for atleast an hour.
  5. Prepare a seperate bowl with eggs. Mix eggs until yolk is dispersed throughout it.
  6. Once marinated, generously dip conch in eggs and allow to be fully covered.
  7. Once conch is drenched with egg, cover with a thin layer of flour (Try using a Ziplock pouch and shaking the flour onto the conch).
  8. Preheat cooking oil in a medium sized pot or deep fryer. Once oil is heated enough begin placing battered clumps of conch into the oil
  9. Wait until conch is golden brown and crispy and then place on a plate or surface with a paper towel on top to let any excess oil drain off.
  10. And there you have it! You can now enjoy your very own cracked conch, Bahamian Style.
