Simple Salad

A simple and healthy food choice.

Here is a recipe for a simple salad.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Bell Pepppers
  • Onions
  • Cucumbers etc...
  • Salad Dressing (Your choice)

Steps

  1. Rinse your vegetables (lettuce, tomatoes, bell peppers, and onions.)
  2. Using a cutting board slice your vegetables into manageable slices (based on your preference).
  3. Place your sliced vegetables into a bowl and dress with a salad dressing of your choice.

As simple as a salad is to make, it adds so many health benefits to your day and is a good balance to any diet.
